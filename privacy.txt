Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Brothex ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our games and applications, including Ram Truck, available on Google Play and other platforms.

Information We Collect

Ram Truck does not collect, store, process, or share any personal information.

The game does not require users to:

Create an account
Provide a name or email address
Share their location
Grant access to contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or personal files

We do not collect any information that can be used to identify you.

Device Permissions

Ram Truck may use your device's vibration or haptic feedback feature to improve gameplay by providing tactile feedback during in-game events.

This feature operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our games may be distributed through third-party platforms such as Google Play. These platforms may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Brothex does not control or have access to information collected by Google Play or other platform providers.

At this time, Ram Truck does not use:

Advertising SDKs
Analytics services
In-app purchases
User accounts or authentication
Cloud storage
Proprietary online servers
Third-party SDKs that collect personal information

If these features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy and our Google Play Data Safety disclosures will be updated before the changes take effect.

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal information through Ram Truck, we do not retain any user data.

If you contact us by email, we may retain your email address and message only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry. You may request deletion of this correspondence at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our games are not specifically directed to children and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Although Ram Truck does not collect personal information, we take reasonable measures to protect any information voluntarily provided when you contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated "Last updated" date.
